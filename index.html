Controls

Touch Controls:

  • Left joystick: Move
  • Right joystick: Aim & shoot
  • B button: Toggle weapon

Keyboard/Mouse:

  • WASD or Arrow keys: Move
  • Mouse: Aim
  • Left click: Shoot
  • Space bar: Toggle weapon

Weapons:

  • GUN: Unlimited ammo, single target
  • BOMB: Limited ammo, area damage
